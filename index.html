What is Distrihub?

Distrihub is a comprehensive, all-in-one ERP system designed as a complete business manager for SMEs in Kenya and East Africa. It empowers owners to run operations efficiently from anywhere, with AI-driven insights and controls that put the business "in their hands" via a mobile-first app.

Distrihub evolves into an AI-driven Business OS where owners chat with their business for advice on routes, budgets, promotions, and more—formalizing informal flows to build health and prosperity.

Expanded Key Features

Business Management

Real-time dashboards for operations, expense tracking (fixed/recurrent), AI flags anomalies (e.g., unusual spending indicating exploitation or false entries), cost optimization advice.

Staff Management

Tracks disciplinary actions, debts (persistent after transfers), advances, salaries. Staff personal accounts for real-time updates. System locks on discrepancies (unpaid debts/unbanked sales), instant owner notifications → reduces risks, promotes accountability.

Stock Management

Strict controls: stock moves only after owner approval (app, audited). Opening balances/direct sales bypass for efficiency. Low-stock alerts, restock requests, POs/GRNs to formalize informal flows (WhatsApp POs to street suppliers or internal save).

POS/Sales & AI

Seamless transactions, real-time reporting, AI route optimization (traffic/weather/sales history), returns. Predictive AI: budgeting, marketing timing, promotions, chat queries ("Is this expense normal?", "Who to promote?").

Supplier, Fleet, Compliance & Payments

  • Supplier pool (Distrihub clients) with portal, AI best-match (distance, cost, reviews).
  • Fleet/Asset: QR codes, depreciation, accounting integration.
  • Compliance: eTIMS auto-registration/submission, statutory reminders, industry-specific scoring, guides unregistered to formalization (nation-building via taxes).
  • Payments: Centralized paybill + instant Daraja API bank transfers (slashes M-Pesa fees, minimal Distrihub charge, reduces cash risks).
  • Bank Partnerships: Secure insights for smarter loans, real-time monitoring, lower defaults, referral revenue.
  • Perks: Loyalty, notifications, webhooks, cheap plans from KSh 500/mo for mama mboga/side hustlers.

The Core Problem Distrihub Solves

  • Lack of Oversight: Tiny losses snowball; approvals, audits, AI flags prevent it.
  • Remote Side Hustles: 71% employed Kenyans run side businesses (often fail from mismanagement); real-time views/locks help.
  • Staff Pilferage/Risks: Theft/debts cause closures; tracking + locks address head-on.
  • Regulatory Burdens: KRA shutdowns common; auto-submissions + scoring guide compliance/formalization.
  • High Costs & Inefficiencies: Transaction fees bleed margins; centralized paybill saves. Asset/expense gaps; QR + AI plug them. Loan barriers; partnerships provide data. Supplier chaos; pooled portals + AI streamline. Informal gaps; gentle POs/GRNs add structure.

Solving these fosters stable jobs, wealth, economic growth—boosting tax contributions and nation-building.
