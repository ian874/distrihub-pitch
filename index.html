The Problem

Kenyan SMEs & side hustles fail at alarming rates (70–80% in first 3–5 years)

  • Overlooked tiny leaks snowball into closure
  • Remote mismanagement for side hustles (71% of employed youth)
  • Staff pilferage, debts, unbanked sales
  • KRA shutdowns & compliance burdens
  • High M-Pesa fees bleed margins
  • No smart promo/bundling tools
  • Informal sector (83.6% jobs) lacks accessible digital tools

The Solution

Distrihub: All-in-one mobile ERP + AI Business OS

  • Real-time dashboards + AI anomaly alerts
  • Owner approvals on stock/expenses (fully audited)
  • eTIMS auto-registration & submission + compliance scoring
  • Centralized paybill → instant bank transfers (cheaper than M-Pesa)
  • Independent sellables with per-item rules
  • Seasonal bundling (auto-dissolve after period)
  • UI-configurable promos (BOGO, flash sales) with tracking
  • Future: Chat AI ("Is this expense normal?", "Best Valentine's bundle?")
